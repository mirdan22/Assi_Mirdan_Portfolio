Cars 4 Sale

My Role: designing, Coding, Research, Photoshoot

Industry: UX/UI, HTML, CSS, JS

Project Type: Buying used cars website

Description

This project was focused on creating a user-friendly online platform for buying and selling used cars. I started with research and planning to gather ideas and information. I designed detailed wireframes and a professional logo using Adobe XD and Illustrator. I also took high-quality photos of cars for the content to enhance the website's appeal. Using HTML5, CSS, and JavaScript.

I developed the website from scratch, ensuring responsiveness and incorporating interactive elements. The final outcome was a thriving online marketplace that exceeded client expectations, providing a secure and seamless experience for both buyers and sellers.
